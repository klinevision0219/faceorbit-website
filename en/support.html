Frequently Asked Questions

Are my facial photos uploaded to a server? +
No. All facial detection and measurement runs entirely on your device. Facial images never leave your phone. We only store the computed numeric results (like a symmetry score), never any images.
How often should I measure? Is there a recommended time? +
We recommend one measurement per day under consistent conditions — for example, after washing your face in the morning, in natural light, facing directly forward. Consistent conditions make long-term trend data more accurate.
How are the 22 metrics calculated? +
Faceorbit uses MediaPipe Face Mesh to detect 468 facial landmark points, then applies geometric calculations (distances, angles, ratios) to produce each metric. This is scientific geometric measurement, not subjective scoring or filter effects.
Where does the crowd benchmark data come from? +
Crowd benchmark data comes from anonymized measurements across all users, grouped by country, gender, and age range. All data is fully anonymized and cannot be traced to any individual user.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → find Faceorbit → Cancel Subscription. You can continue using the app until the end of the current billing period. All subscriptions are managed through Apple — Faceorbit does not handle payments directly.
How do I delete all my data? +
You can select "Delete All Records" in the app settings, or email faceorbittw@gmail.com to request complete deletion of your account and all associated data. We'll process your request within 7 business days.
What languages does the app support? +
Faceorbit currently supports Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese (日本語), and Korean (한국어). More languages may be added in future updates.
