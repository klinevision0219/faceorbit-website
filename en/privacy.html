Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Our core commitment: All facial detection and measurement processing runs entirely on your device. Faceorbit does not collect, upload, or store any facial images on external servers.

What We Collect

Faceorbit collects the following types of data to provide its services:

  • Facial measurement values (numbers, not images): Computed results from 22 structural metrics, stored on your device and in our cloud database for trend tracking and benchmark comparison.
  • Device identifier: Used to link consecutive measurements from the same user, not connected to personal identity.
  • Demographic information (optional): Country, age group, and gender — used solely for anonymous crowd benchmark comparisons.
  • App usage analytics: Anonymous statistics such as feature usage frequency and crash reports, used to improve the product.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Facial photos or images — all images are processed in real-time in device memory and immediately discarded
  • Your name, email, or any directly identifying information (unless you contact support)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or data from other apps

How Facial Detection Works

Faceorbit uses MediaPipe Face Mesh to detect 468 facial landmark points entirely on your device. Only the computed numeric results (e.g., symmetry index = 0.92) are uploaded — never any form of image.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To provide personal trend charts and personal best tracking
  • To compare your metrics against anonymous benchmarks grouped by country and age
  • To improve algorithm accuracy and app stability

Data Storage and Security

Your measurement values are stored in a secure cloud database provided by Supabase, using industry-standard encryption. All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third-party marketing organizations.

Subscriptions and Payments

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. Faceorbit does not store any payment information. To manage, cancel, or request refunds for subscriptions, please use your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

Faceorbit is not designed for or marketed to children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your region). If you believe a minor has used the app without guardian consent, please contact us and we will delete the relevant data.

Your Rights

  • Delete all personal measurement records in the app settings at any time
  • Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Stop using and uninstall the app at any time

Policy Changes

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you in the app and update the "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
