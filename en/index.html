Core Philosophy

Rather than chasing a single "perfect" score, Faceorbit focuses on two things: your facial symmetry and the stability of long-term change. Understanding your own baseline and tracking your direction matters more than any external standard.

Three Analysis Dimensions

22 Metrics Total

From symmetry and facial proportions to early aging signals — your face, fully measured.

⚖️
Dimension 01
Balance
  • Midline deviation — left-right structural gap
  • Brow-eye distance — fatigue and expression habits
  • Mouth corner height difference — smile asymmetry quantified
  • Jaw angle difference — structural symmetry detection
📐
Dimension 02
Proportion
  • Jaw arc radius — the basic curve of your face shape
  • Cheekbone-to-jaw ratio — key driver of first impressions
  • Philtrum ratio — golden ratio clues in the details
  • Jaw firmness — early changes in jawline definition
⏱️
Dimension 03
Aging
  • Nasolabial fold depth — one of the earliest aging signals
  • Cheek displacement — changes in facial support
  • Marionette lines — now trackable for the first time

Why Faceorbit

Science-based. Self-focused.

Not subjective scoring — a long-term tracking system grounded in geometric measurement.

🏆

Personal Best, Always Saved

Every metric automatically records your historical best value and the date it was achieved, along with the gap from today. The most direct proof of your own progress.

👥

Crowd Benchmark Comparison

Your data is compared in real-time against anonymized benchmarks from the same country and age group. Not a beauty contest — an objective coordinate to understand where you stand.

📈

Trend Tracking

Each metric shows an "Above / Below / Stable" status indicator so you can see at a glance which direction your face is heading — not just a single snapshot, but a trend curve over time.

🔒

Fully On-Device Processing

All facial detection and measurement runs entirely on your device. Your facial images never leave your phone. Privacy stays with you.
